I am a Web Developer at Full Circle with full stack experience and a focus on the front-end, working mostly with Backbone, Marionette and Ruby on Rails.

Concurrently, I co-founded Up Dog Toys, which successfully launched a toy on Kickstarter in November 2014. I am also a co-founder of t+j designs, a rapidly growing fashion jewelry brand, and Philanthro Seattle, a non-profit dedicated to getting young professionals involved in philanthropy. I've done web development for all of the above organizations.

Lastly, I also worked at 2 start-ups (Widemile/Webtrends, and Realself) doing online marketing and product development.
